How much does it cost?

We have a free plan and paid plans. The plan you subscribe to determines the number of days you will receive daily tips and advice.

Plan Price
Free (1 free advice per week) Kes 0
1 Month Kes 30
6 Months + 15 Days Free Kes 180
1 Year + 2 Months Free Kes 360

Do you deduct money from my airtime?

We do not deduct money from your airtime.

How do I subscribe or renew my plan?

To subscribe or renew your plan; send the amount corresponding to your preferred plan to Paybill 961700 Account 588.
Example: To subscribe or renew the 6 Months plan send Kes 180 to Paybill 961700 Account 588

How is the expiry date calculated?

If let's say you subscribe to the 1 Month plan today, your expiry date will be 30 days from today. The 6 Months and 1 Year plans delay your plan expiry by adding an extra 15 days and 2 months respectively.

What happens when my plan expires?

When your plan expires you will be automatically transitioned to the free plan. You will have to renew your preferred plan to continue receiving daily tips and advice.
