How it works

  1. Download and install Notisha.
  2. Register by providing your name, relationship status and mobile number.
  3. Seat back and receive the best tips and advice from leading marriage counsellors and relationship experts.
You will receive one relationship tip or marriage advice daily. Members on the free plan will receive one free tip or advice per week.

How much does it cost?

We have a free plan and paid plans. The plan you subscribe to determines the number of days you will receive daily tips and advice.

Plan Price
Free Kes 0
1 Month Kes 30
6 Months + 15 Days Free Kes 180
1 Year + 2 Months Free Kes 360

Do you deduct money from my airtime?

No. To subscribe or renew your plan; send the amount corresponding to your preferred plan to Paybill Number 961700 Account Number 588, from your Safaricom line.

How is the expiry date calculated?

If let's say you subscribe to the 1 Month plan today, your expiry date will be 30 days from today.
The 6 Months and 1 Year plans delay your plan expiry by adding an extra 15 days and 2 months respectively.

What happens when my plan expires?

When your plan expires you will be automatically transitioned to the free plan. You will have to renew your preferred plan to continue receiving daily tips and advice.
