How it works

  1. Download and install Notisha.
  2. Register by providing your name and mobile number.
  3. Seat back and receive the best tips and advice on a daily basis.
You will receive one marital or parenting advice on daily basis. Members on the free plan will receive one free advice per week.
You are eligible to 7 days of free advice when you download and install Notisha.

How much does it cost?

We have a free plan and paid plans. The plan you subscribe to determines the number of days you will receive daily tips and advice.

Plan Price
Free Kes 0
1 Month Kes 30
6 Months + 15 Days Free Kes 180
1 Year + 2 Months Free Kes 360

Do you deduct money from my airtime?

We do not deduct money from your airtime. To subscribe or renew your plan; send the amount corresponding to your preferred plan to 0712 688 559 (MPESA) or 0739 555 719 (Airtel Money).

How is the expiry date calculated?

If let's say you subscribe to the 1 Month plan today, your expiry date will be 30 days from today. The 6 Months and 1 Year plans delay your plan expiry by adding an extra 15 days and 2 months respectively.

What happens when my plan expires?

When your plan expires you will be automatically transitioned to the free plan. You will have to renew your preferred plan to continue receiving daily tips and advice.

When are you going to make an app for the Web, iOs and Windows?

We are currently working on making the Android experience as solid as possible. Next on our product pipeline will be to build for the most requested platform. You may place your request via the form below.
